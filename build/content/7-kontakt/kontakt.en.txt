Auto: You can also reach our book store by car via Wallstraße, turning into Marienstraße, although you are only allowed to halt for quick loading and unloading. For parking, check out the parking garages Schlossberg and Schwabentor (within five minutes walking distance).

----

Bike: The Fundevogel book store is centrally located in Freiburg's picturesque Old Town, only two minutes afoot from Augustinerplatz. From there you go past the famous Feierling Brewery, turn right and cross the bridge spanning the Gewerbebach (beware of the crocodile). Following the street you can see our book store on the left, opposite to the (link: http://www.freiburg.de/pb/,Len/237848.html text: Museum für Neue Kunst popup: yes).

----

Tram: Or you just take the tram - the nearest tram stops are „Bertoldsbrunnen“, „Holzmarkt“ (multiple tramlines) and „Oberlinden“(tramline 1).
