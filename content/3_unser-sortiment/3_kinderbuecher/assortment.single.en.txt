## Stories about life
First-time readers will find wonderful books on our shelves, such as the exciting stories about the lovable “Detective Gordon” and the school friend stories about "Dunne" by Rose Lagercrantz:

With large letters, simple, clear texts and imaginative illustrations, they just make you want to start reading right away!
